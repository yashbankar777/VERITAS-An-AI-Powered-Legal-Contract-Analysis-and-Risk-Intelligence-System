Intelligence Architecture

The VERITAS
Analysis Stack

Three distinct intelligence layers operating in concert to produce institutional-grade contract analysis. Each layer contributes a distinct form of reasoning to the composite result.

Layer I

Neural Classification

Legal-BERT Transformer Model

A fine-tuned transformer model trained on legal corpora identifies and classifies clause types with high precision. The model assigns confidence scores reflecting classification certainty and provides the foundational typology for downstream reasoning.

Clause segmentation Type classification Confidence scoring
feeds into
Layer II

Symbolic Reasoning

Ontology & Knowledge Representation

A formal legal ontology enriches classified clauses with structured knowledge about legal concepts, dependencies, and risk domains. Knowledge representation and reasoning (KRR) methods surface latent contractual relationships and multi-domain exposure vectors.

Ontology enrichment Risk domain mapping Dependency analysis
feeds into
Layer III

Generative Explanation

Large Language Model Synthesis

A large language model synthesises the structured outputs of Layers I and II into executive-grade natural language. Plain English summaries, legal implications, business implications, and risk interpretations are generated for each clause and the contract as a whole.

Plain English translation Legal implications Business advisory
